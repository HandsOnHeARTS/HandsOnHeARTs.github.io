About Us

The Hands on HeARTs club can be described in three words: Creative, Excited, and Passionate. We welcome all students, regardless of artistic talent, and support their creative interests. With a variety of arts and crafts projects, the Hands on HeARTs club is the perfect club for students looking to improve their work or learn a new art style or technique. Featured on this website are some of our previous projects. Feel free to browse through our collection!
